FeedReady
Clean your image, not your creativity.
How it works
1
Click "Get image post-ready" in the green bar above your LinkedIn post composer
2
Pick your image — FeedReady optimises it for LinkedIn compatibility and downloads a clean copy
3
Upload the _feedready file to LinkedIn and post as normal
Uses canvas image processing — 100% local, nothing leaves your device.
FeedReady improves image compatibility and removes unnecessary metadata. We support responsible AI use. Please use this tool ethically and be transparent about AI-generated content where appropriate.
